Domino's Headquarters in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Domino's Pizza, Inc. is a renowned American multinational pizza restaurant chain founded in 1960 by Tom Monaghan and James Monaghan. Originally, it was named DomiNick's but was later renamed Domino's Pizza in 1965. Headquartered in Ann Arbor, Michigan, Domino's has grown into one of the largest pizza chains globally.
    Domino's is famous for its wide variety of pizzas, including traditional flavors like pepperoni, cheese, and sausage, as well as unique offerings such as the ExtravaganZZa and Honolulu Hawaiian. Additionally, the company offers various side dishes like breadsticks, chicken wings, and desserts like chocolate lava cakes.
   With thousands of locations worldwide, Domino's continues to expand its global presence while maintaining its reputation
delicious pizza and efficient service.
